## Why this mattered

The feature itself was small, but it forced me to think about edge cases, loading states, and naming.

## What changed in my workflow

- I started listing UI states before coding.
- I wrote smaller commits with clearer intent.
- I tested the failure path instead of assuming the happy path worked.

## What I would do next

I would define acceptance criteria first and use that to shape the PR description.
