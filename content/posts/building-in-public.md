## The main reason

I wanted a place where my work, lessons, and mistakes could all be visible in one place.

## What writing changes

Writing forces me to explain tradeoffs, not just outcomes. That makes projects easier to present and easier to revisit later.

## What comes next

I want to connect related posts better so readers can move through topics instead of reading one article in isolation.
